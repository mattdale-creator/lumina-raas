Lumina RaaS Moat / Differentiation

  • Results-as-a-Service model — No competitor offers pay-only-on-verified-delivery. This eliminates customer risk entirely.
  • 12-PRD full lifecycle — Competitors handle pieces; Lumina covers everything from marketing site to monitoring.
  • Ralph loops with explicit success criteria — Self-improving code that iterates until measurable targets are met. Prevents the "off-track" problem that plagues Devin.
  • Aether autonomous sales agent — No competitor includes a customer acquisition tool. Lumina sells itself.
  • Outcome-based pricing — Aligned incentives: Lumina only profits when the customer succeeds.
