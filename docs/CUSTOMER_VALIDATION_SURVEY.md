### Analysis Framework

After collecting responses, score and segment:
- **Hot leads:** Q4 ≤ 4 AND Q7 ≥ 7 AND Q8 = "Yes, immediately"
- **Warm leads:** Q7 ≥ 5 AND Q10 ≥ "$49–$99"
- **Key insights:** Aggregate Q5 for top pain point, Q10 for pricing validation, Q12 for vertical priority
